About BAFAW

BAFAW is a Milwaukee-based nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting the Somali Bantu community through advocacy, services, and community-led initiatives.

Mission

To empower Somali Bantu families through advocacy, services, and programs that build opportunity and wellbeing.

Vision

A safe, thriving, respected community where families have access to support and a strong voice.

Values

Service, unity, integrity, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.

Programs & Services

We provide support across multiple areas. If you need help, contact us and we will guide you to the right service.

Human Rights Advocacy

Support, referrals, and community education to protect civil rights and promote fair access.

Resettlement Support

Guidance, local navigation, and connecting families to schools, housing resources, and community services.

Community Services

Forms help, referrals, case support, and connecting families to trusted local providers.

Youth Sports

Sports programs and mentorship promoting health, leadership, teamwork, and confidence.

Family & Children Services

Support and referrals for families, children, and youth wellbeing.

Community Engagement

Outreach, workshops, partnerships, and community-led initiatives that strengthen community voice.
